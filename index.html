Protect LGBTQ+ Youth

Preventing the discrimination and abuse that many LGBTQ+ youth face around the world.

LGBTQ+ youth face an immense amount of stigma from society during their developmental years. This includes verbal harrasement and hate speech, as well as physical assault and hate crimes. Change is necessary in order to allow for the safety of these youth and let them be their most authentic selves, free of judgement.
