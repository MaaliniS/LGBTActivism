How you can help.

You can visit The Trevor Project website, who's work provides valuable resources to young people nationwide who may not have anywhere else to turn to for help.


Sign as many petitions as possible, regarding the protection of LGBTQ+ youth.


If you are not a member of the LGBTQ+ community, be a good ally. You can read up on how to be a better ally here.
